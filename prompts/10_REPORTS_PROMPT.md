# Increo Reports & Analytics Module Implementation Prompt

Version: 1.0

---

# Objective

Your responsibility is to design and implement the complete Reports & Analytics module for the Increo platform.

This module is not simply responsible for displaying charts.

Its responsibility is to transform salary planning data into meaningful business intelligence that enables managers, HR, and directors to make informed compensation decisions.

Reports should answer business questions.

Reports should identify trends.

Reports should detect anomalies.

Reports should estimate payroll impact.

Reports should support strategic decision making.
